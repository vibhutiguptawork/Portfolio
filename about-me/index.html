ABOUT ME.

In my journey as a designer and technologist, Ive worked across UI/UX, web and mobile apps, and brand design for companies in fashion, skincare, and fintech. Im driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and the desire to create meaningful digital experiences.

With a strong engineering foundation, I believe good design goes beyond aesthetics it must be intuitive, accessible, and genuinely useful. Im constantly learning, evolving, and seeking opportunities to create thoughtful, user-centric solutions.
